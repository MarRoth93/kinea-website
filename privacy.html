1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to:

  • The Kinea website at kinea-app.com (including the password reset landing page)
  • The Kinea mobile app
  • Our backend API and related services used to provide Kinea

Third-party platforms (for example, Google Play) have their own privacy policies and terms. This policy does not cover their practices.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide, information generated by your use of Kinea, and limited information from third parties.

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account information: email address, username (if you choose one), and authentication data (password is stored as a hash, not in plain text).
  • Profile & fitness context (optional): goal (e.g., hypertrophy/strength), gender, birth date, height, weight, and preferences.
  • Workout & training data: workouts, exercises, sets (e.g., weight, reps), timestamps, and any notes you add.
  • Social features (“Gym Buddies”) data: friend connections, friend requests, and in-app notifications you send/receive.
  • Content you upload: if you upload PDFs/papers for research features, we process and store that content.
  • Support communications: messages you send to us (and the information you include) when requesting help.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device and app information: device type, OS version, app version, language/locale, and similar technical details.
  • Log and diagnostic data: IP address, timestamps, requested pages/endpoints, and basic request metadata (for security and reliability).

2.3 Subscriptions and Advertising

  • Subscription status: we maintain a premium/non-premium status for your account and related entitlement information.
  • Purchases: purchases are processed by app store providers (e.g., Google Play) and/or subscription management providers (e.g., RevenueCat). We generally do not receive your full payment card details.
  • Rewarded ads (if enabled): when you watch a rewarded ad, advertising providers (e.g., Google AdMob) may collect device identifiers (such as the advertising ID), ad interaction data, and related technical information to provide and verify the reward.

2.4 Information From Other Users (When You Use Social Features)

If you use “Gym Buddies”, other users may share content with you such as PR notifications and reactions.

3. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve Kinea (including syncing your workouts across devices).
  • Generate training recommendations, volume analysis, and recovery insights based on your workout history and selected settings.
  • Enable social features (friend requests, comparisons, notifications) when you opt in.
  • Manage premium access, free-tier limits, and rewarded-ad credits.
  • Send transactional emails (e.g., password reset and verification).
  • Prevent fraud and abuse, enforce our Terms, and protect the security and integrity of Kinea.

4. Legal Bases (EEA/UK)

This section applies if you are in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom (UK). If you are elsewhere, legal bases may differ.

We process personal information under one or more of the following legal bases:

  • Contract: to provide the Service you request (e.g., create an account, store workouts, sync data, deliver recommendations).
  • Legitimate interests: to secure, maintain, and improve the Service (e.g., prevent abuse, troubleshoot, and maintain reliability), where our interests are not overridden by your rights.
  • Consent: when required (e.g., for certain optional features, or where local law treats certain fitness/profile inputs as sensitive).
  • Legal obligations: to comply with applicable laws and enforce our Terms.

5. How We Share Information

We share information only as described below.

4.1 With Service Providers (Processors)

We use third-party vendors to help deliver Kinea. Depending on your use of features and our configuration, these vendors may process limited information on our behalf:

  • Hosting and security: Cloudflare (website delivery/security) and infrastructure providers (e.g., Hetzner) for servers and storage.
  • Email delivery: Resend (to send password reset and verification emails).
  • Subscriptions: RevenueCat and app stores (e.g., Google Play) to manage entitlements and purchase status.
  • Advertising (rewarded ads): Google AdMob (to show rewarded ads and verify rewards).
  • AI providers (for recommendations/analysis): Google (Gemini for generation and Google embeddings for retrieval).

Note: Kinea’s evidence base (scientific papers) may be stored in our vector database for retrieval; this is used to generate citations and evidence-based recommendations.

4.2 With Other Users (Only When You Choose to Use Social Features)

If you use “Gym Buddies”, some of your information is shared with your selected friends in order to provide the feature. This can include your username and email address, workout comparison stats (e.g., total volume, total sets), and PR shares (e.g., exercise, weight, reps, date), depending on what you share.

Friend discovery: other users can send you a friend request by searching for your username or email address. If someone knows your email address, they may be able to find your account through this feature.

4.3 Legal, Safety, and Compliance

We may disclose information if required by law or if we reasonably believe disclosure is necessary to protect rights, safety, and security, investigate fraud, or enforce our Terms.

4.4 We Do Not Sell Your Personal Information

We do not sell your personal information in exchange for money. We also do not share your personal information for “cross-context behavioral advertising” in a way that would be considered a “sale” or “sharing” under certain privacy laws, except to the extent that third-party ad technology providers may collect information directly through their SDKs (for example, AdMob) when you use rewarded ads.

6. AI / Automated Processing

Kinea can generate recommendations and insights using AI models. To do this, we may send limited context to AI providers, such as your recent workout summaries (exercise names, sets, reps, weights, and dates), your goal, and relevant evidence retrieved from scientific literature.

AI outputs can be inaccurate or incomplete. You are responsible for how you use recommendations, and you should use good judgment and consult a professional when appropriate.

We do not use AI to make decisions that produce legal effects or similarly significant impacts on you.

7. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide Kinea and for legitimate business purposes (such as security and dispute resolution).

  • Account, workouts, and recommendations: retained until you delete them or delete your account.
  • Our application/server logs: typically retained up to 30 days for security and troubleshooting, unless a longer period is required to investigate abuse, comply with law, or resolve disputes.
  • Subscription and fraud-prevention records: may be retained longer as required by law, platform policies, or for accounting/security purposes.

If you delete your account, we delete or de-identify associated data from our active systems, subject to necessary retention for legal/security reasons and limitations of third-party systems (e.g., app stores, subscription providers, or hosting/security providers).

8. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect information. No system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
